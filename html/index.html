About me

At 23 I was working a manual labor job and attempting college unfulfilled. I decided that the path I was on didn't align with my goals and with a giant leap of faith used a credit card with a $500 limit to start a small home service based company. Within 3 years the company went from $0 in revenue per year to over $400,000. I consider the experience and education I got from starting my own company to be priceless, and as of January 2019 I sold the company giving myself a tremendous amount of freedom. I believe irreverence is a virtue and that there are many paths to accomplishing goals so long as you keep an open mind and are willing to work like a mercenary.
